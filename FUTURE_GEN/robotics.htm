Robotics Technology

Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with designing, building and operating robots that can perform tasks automatically. Robots are used in industries, healthcare, space exploration, and even in daily life.

What is Robotics?

Robotics combines engineering, electronics, and AI to create intelligent machines that can perform human-like tasks.

Robotics Research

Scientists are developing advanced robots that can walk, talk, think, and interact with humans.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. It enables systems to learn, reason, and make decisions. AI is transforming industries like healthcare, education, transportation, and business.

What is AI?

AI allows machines to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. It includes technologies like Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Neural Networks.

AI Research

Researchers are developing advanced AI systems that can understand language, recognize images, and even make predictions.

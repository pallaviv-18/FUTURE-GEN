Green Energy

Green Energy is the future of sustainable living. It comes from renewable sources like solar, wind, hydro and biomass, helping reduce pollution and protect nature.

Solar Energy

Solar energy is generated from sunlight using solar panels. It is clean, renewable and widely used for electricity production.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is produced using wind turbines. It is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy sources worldwide.

Why Green Energy?

🌍 Eco Friendly

Reduces pollution and protects the environment.

⚡ Renewable

Never runs out like fossil fuels.

💡 Future Power

Will power future smart cities and industries.
